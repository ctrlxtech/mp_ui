CAN I RE-BOOK WITH THE SAME MASSAGE THERAPIST?

Yes, you can! After your first massage with any MassagePanda therapist, you will be able to re-book future massages with the same therapist by indicating the name of the therapist in the special instruction section.

Please keep in mind that we cannot guarantee that the same therapist will be available at the time you choose. But we would be happy to discuss other options with you or rearrange your booking.

WHAT TYPES OF SERVICES ARE OFFERED BY MASSAGEPANDA?

MassagePanda currently offers Swedish massage and deep tissue massage. We will offer other types of massage in the future.

Any of these modalities can be performed as a Couples Massage, which means that two MassagePanda therapists will perform massages for you and your spouse/partner/friend at the same time. Please indicate Couples Massage in the special instruction section during your booking.

MassagePanda also offers corporate massage service, in which you can select any number of therapists for a period of time up to 6 hours per therapist for any of the modalities listed above, as well as chair massage, which is great for treating your office for example. Please contact us for more details.

WHAT ABOUT PARKING?

When booking, we ask that you provide as detailed parking information as you have available. If our therapists are unable to find free parking, or none is available in your area, we kindly ask that you reimburse the therapist for any expenses incurred, such as valet expenses if you are staying at a hotel.

WHAT ARE MASSAGEPANDA'S HOURS OF OPERATION?

MassagePanda offers massages 7 days a week from 9 am to 9 pm. These hours refer to first and last available appointment start time.

WHAT IS MASSAGEPANDA'S CANCELLATION POLICY?

Once an appointment is confirmed, you are notified via e-mail and text. We understand that plans can change, so if you need to cancel your appointment, you can do so within 10 minutes of confirmation with no penalty. After the initial 10 minute period, unfortunately, we have to charge a $50 penalty for any cancellation that takes place less than 4 hours prior to the scheduled massage time. For massages that are cancelled less than 1 hour from the scheduled appointment time, we charge the full amount of the massage. We put these cancellation policies in place to protect our therapists, as they lose income when cancellations occur, and need to receive compensation for time they have set aside.

DO I NEED MY OWN MASSAGE TABLE AND SHEETS?

No! MassagePanda therapists always come prepared with massage table, fresh linens, and essential oil, etc.

However, if you have your own massage table or linens that you prefer, please indicate in the special instruction section during your booking and our therapists are happy to use yours.

WHAT SHOULD I WEAR DURING THE MASSAGE SESSION?

It doesn't matter at all what you wear. During the session, your preference for your state of undress depends on your comfort level. However, it will feel best for the client to be completely unclothed, for the creams and oils to fully glide on the skin, and the session to flow like a dance. There will be draping to always protect the modesty of the client.

HOW OFTEN SHOULD I GET A MASSAGE?

Listen to your body. Once a week, twice a month, whenever, you feel like it is time.
